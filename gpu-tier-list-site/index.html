Introduction to the Tierlist

This GPU Tierlist is based purely on my subjective opinion, considering factors like raw performance, value for money, and future-proofing. It's intended to give you a general idea rather than strict benchmarks. Remember, GPU performance can vary with different games, applications, and system configurations.

Featured GPU: NVIDIA GeForce RTX 4090

The RTX 4090 stands at the pinnacle of consumer GPUs, offering unparalleled performance for 4K gaming and demanding creative workloads. While expensive, its raw power is unmatched, making it the choice for enthusiasts who demand the absolute best.

Tier S - Unrivaled Performance

RTX 4090

Absolute beast for everything. Dominates 4K gaming and professional tasks with ease.

RX 7900 XTX

Great AMD high-end option. Offers strong rasterization performance and competitive pricing in its class.

Tier A - Excellent Performance

RTX 4080 SUPER

Strong performer, better value than original 4080. A solid choice for high refresh rate 1440p and respectable 4K gaming.

RX 7900 XT

Another strong AMD contender. Provides excellent performance for its price point, especially in demanding games.

RTX 3090 Ti

Still very capable, but power hungry. If you can find one at a good price, it's still a powerhouse for creative work and gaming.

Tier B - Strong Mid-High Range

RTX 4070 SUPER

Excellent 1440p gaming GPU with good ray tracing capabilities and DLSS 3 support.

RX 7800 XT

A fantastic value proposition for 1440p gaming, offering strong raw performance.

RTX 3080 / 3080 Ti

Still very capable cards for 1440p and even some 4K gaming, especially if found at a good used price.

Tier C - Solid Mid-Range

RTX 4060 Ti

Good for 1080p and decent for 1440p with DLSS. A power-efficient option.

RX 7700 XT

Strong competitor at its price point for high refresh rate 1080p and capable 1440p gaming.

RTX 3060 Ti / RX 6700 XT

Excellent choices for 1080p gaming even today, and capable of some 1440p. Great value for budget builds.
